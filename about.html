MASK

MASK are a two piece noise rock duo from the Bedford/ Milton Keynes area. Taking influences from Death From Above 2979, '68, and advertise themselves as a "heavier version of Royal Blood". The bands hard, groovy riffs mixed with their big atmospheric sound, original yet nostalgic aesthetic, memorable tunes and dedication to their performance live makes them a band to see this year.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is for preexisting fans and new customers to be able to see merchandise and music available from the band and provide links to socials and music streaming platforms to increase click through rate.

Target Audience

As this band is a small, local grass root band, the target audience for this band is nation wide. This being the band can take advantage of a wider reach, allowing for progressive growth in audience. It would be unreasonable for this websites target audience to be any larger than the UK due to scaling with shipment and currency conversion.
